# Comparative Study of Mumbai–Pune and Bengaluru–Tamil Nadu Industrial Regions
## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comparative study of two major industrial regions in India: Mumbai–Pune and Bengaluru–Tamil Nadu. It analyzes their historical development, industrial structure, infrastructure, and contribution to economic growth.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To study how geographical factors influence industrial location
- To analyze the role of government policies
- To compare employment generation and regional development

---

## 📍 Study Area
- Mumbai–Pune Industrial Region (Maharashtra)
- Bengaluru–Tamil Nadu Industrial Corridor

---

## 🧪 Methodology
- Secondary data analysis
- Government reports (ASI, Economic Survey)
- Literature review
- Comparative analysis

---

## 📊 Key Findings
- Mumbai–Pune is dominated by traditional industries like automobiles and petrochemicals
- Bengaluru–Tamil Nadu is driven by IT, electronics, and innovation
- Infrastructure and policy play a major role in industrial growth
- Both regions contribute significantly to employment and GDP

---
